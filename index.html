Traffic crash data from Chicago

By Alexia Kadota-Browner, Tom O'Connor, Janani Jana

From June 4th 2018 to October 9th 2023, 99 crashes were reported in the city of Chicago.

Most crashes did not produce any injuries.

Despite a low injury rate, most crashes were costly, totaling in over $1,500 of damages.

Surprisingly, weather did not seem to influence the number of crashes much, with most occuring during clear conditions.
