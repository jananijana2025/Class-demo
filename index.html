Traffic crash data from Chicago

By Alexia Kadota-Browner, Tom O'Connor & Janani Janarthanan

From January 1, 2025 to January 31 2025, 8178 crashes were reported in the city of Chicago.

Most crashes did not produce any injuries.

Despite a low injury rate, most crashes were costly, totaling in over $1,500 of damages.

Surprisingly, snowny weather accounted for only 12% of the crashes with clear weather reported on a higher number of clear weather.
